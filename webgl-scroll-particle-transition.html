Three.js Particle Transition

Scroll to shatter the image into living pixels

This starts as one coherent image mapped on a point cloud. As scroll progress increases, each point is displaced with 3D noise and radial burst vectors, while the camera zooms through the cloud.

The viewport emerges into new content

Once particles fly past the camera, standard DOM content fades in. This keeps the transition cinematic while preserving regular HTML/CSS layout for accessibility and maintainability.

Performance: particle count is capped and downsampled from source image pixels.
Resizing: camera aspect and renderer dimensions update on every viewport change.
A11y: prefers-reduced-motion bypasses heavy motion and shows static content.
